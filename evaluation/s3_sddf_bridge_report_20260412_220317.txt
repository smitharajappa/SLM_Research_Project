  METHODOLOGY
  ------------------------------------------------------------------
  S3 (Top-Down): Expert scores 6 dimensions (TC, OS, SK, DS, LT, VL)
    → Computes S3 = sum(Score_i * w_i) / sum(5 * w_i) * 5
    → Assigns tier: Pure SLM (<3.2), Hybrid (3.2-4.0), LLM Only (>4.0)

  SDDF (Bottom-Up): Empirical difficulty scoring + routing evaluation
    → Models: qwen2.5 (0.5B, 3B, 7B) + Llama-3.3-70B baseline
    → Metrics: SLM coverage (% queries routed locally), capability, risk
    → Wilson Score CI for routing thresholds

  Bridge Hypothesis: S3 score should negatively correlate with SDDF
  SLM routing coverage. Low S3 (simple tasks) → high SLM coverage.
  High S3 (complex tasks) → low SLM coverage, LLM-dependent.
